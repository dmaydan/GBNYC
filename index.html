NYC Charitable Organizations

GiveBackNYC.org allows New Yorkers to find charitable organizations that are looking for volunteers. As a high-school student in the Bronx, it has been difficult for me to discover organizations whose messages I really resonate with. I hope this website will help other high-school students make an impact by giving back to our amazing city. Almost 200 organizations are split up into 5 categories based on the types of volunteering opportunities offered.
